Advantages
^^^^^^^^^^

The advantages of the one-click-installation is:

- You will have a minimum of costs for the installation and the configuration:
- You will need 

  - the first time 30 minutes over all and
  - all next times 15 minutes over all.

- You will get a running sample ready-to-use with 

  - 16 pages and folders
  - 6 items
  - 6 item categories
  - 5 content elements
  - 3 plugins
  - 3 TypoScript templates
  - and without
  - any unproper configuration and
  - any costs for debugging.

- A running sample is the best for understanding.
- You can test all features of the Quick Shop, Caddy and Powermail immediately. Play with all!

If something goes wrong and you can't cancel it, don't panic!

You can repeat the one-click-installation unlimited.
